DJI Mavic Air

About

Hi! My name is Judit, I live in Bratislava, Slovakia. I focus on aerial photography projects in the countries of Central Europe. As a hobby photographer I am interested in architecture and in relationship between man and nature. I use a DJI Mavic Air drone, which is a very reliable and durable machine.

The DJI Mavic Air features a fully stabilized 3-axis gimbal camera, capable of shooting 4K video and 12 megapixel photos. The maximum flight speed of the drone is 68 km/h and a maximum flight time is 21 minutes.

Aircraft:

Weight: 430 g

Dimensions: 168×184×64 mm (L×W×H)

Maximum Takeoff Altitude: 5000 m

Max Flight Distance (no wind): 10 km

GNSS: GPS + GLONASS

Internal Storage: 8GB

Camera:

Sensor: 1/2.3” CMOS - 12 MP

Lens: 24 mm, f/2.8

ISO range: 100 - 3200

Image Size: 4056×3040 px

Video Resolution: 4K Ultra HD: 3840×2160

Photo Format: JPEG/DNG (RAW)

Flight Battery:

Capacity: 2375 mAh

Voltage: 11.55 V

Max Charging Power: 60 W

Battery Type: LiPo 3S
